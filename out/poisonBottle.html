Class: poisonBottle

poisonBottle(x, y)

Represents a poison bottle in the game that can animate through a series of images to show its content dynamically. Extends MovableObject to utilize basic movement and animation functionalities.

Constructor

new poisonBottle(x, y)

Constructs a poison bottle with a specified position and preloads images for animation.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
x number The initial horizontal position of the poison bottle.
y number The initial vertical position of the poison bottle.
